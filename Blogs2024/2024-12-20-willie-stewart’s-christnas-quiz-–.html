Willie Stewart’s Christnas Quiz

  • No comments

On 17 December 2024, Willie Stewart presented his well-kown style of quiz, having done it for over twenty years. The wide range of questions tripped up most people, but the star of the evening was Brian Hignett.

Brian is pictured receiving a fine bottle of wine from President Colin.
